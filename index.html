Research Interests

I am deeply passionate about Physical Design, ASIC methodology, and FPGA-based system design. My research interests focus on improving RTL-to-GDSII flows, floorplanning, power planning, timing closure, and optimization using automated scripting (TCL) and advanced design methodologies. I am highly motivated to contribute to the VLSI field, constantly learning and experimenting to push the boundaries of ASIC and FPGA design. My goal is to pursue a PhD at an esteemed university to research and develop next-generation physical design solutions that improve efficiency and performance multifold.
